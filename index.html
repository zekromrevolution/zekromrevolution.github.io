Server Rules

Please take a moment to look through our server rules.
The Rules provided below contain full information about each rule...

Basic Rules

01. Rules apply everywhere in the server at all times. All rules include but are not limited to their meaning and understanding. Ignoring or not reading these rules does not make you immune to them. Not all rules are listed here, so please use some common sense, and don't do something which make us take any action against you.

02. The Discord Terms of Service (https://discord.com/terms) and Guidelines (https://discord.com/guidelines) will always apply.

03. No Punishment Evasion. Users should not attempt to evade the consequences of their actions by any means, including but not limited to: using alts (alternative accounts), leaving/re-joining the server, lying, etc.

04. Please take a moment in reading all rules to keep yourself safe.

01. Respect Everyone

1.1 Don't be toxic —
Witch hunting, discrimination, sexism or racism will not be tolerated. Respect our server staff, members and our helpers; do not be toxic towards anyone in the server. Remember that everyone is human, just like you. Be considerate to all members, and do not attempt to troll or raid - these activities can result in strict consequences against you.

1.2 Respect everyone's privacy —
Do not invade anyone's privacy. Hence, ALL forms of doxxing (leaking sensitive info without prior consent) or other attempts to undermine the character of any party are strictly prohibited. This also means that do cannot try to force anyone into giving their info by the use of IP grabbers or any other similar means. This also includes genders; don't force anyone to tell what their gender is if they don't want to tell, we all are human.

1.3 Respect everyone's safety —
Harmful files or links are not allowed, even as a joke. Do not attempt trick people in something that could be unsafe for them.

02. No NSFW/ NSFL

2.1 Absolutely no NSFW or NSFL content will be tolerated. This includes NSFW in form of text, images, GIFs, links, emotes or anything similar. Adult jokes or any reference to NSFW is not allowed.

03. No Advertising & Begging

3.1 Advertising is strictly prohibited. This includes advertising in DMs, through bots, links, or any other means.

3.2 Don't beg for any kind of in-
Game or other things like Nitro, Redeem Codes or anything. It includes in-game bots economy/items such as OwO, Dank, Pokécoins etc.

04. Profile Rules

4.1 Your profile picture and display name should also be in accordance with the server rules. This means any inappropriate profile pictures or display names are prohibited.

05. Text Channel Rules

5.1 Do not discuss offensive/controversial materials. This includes topics such as politics, religion, acts of violence, suicide/self-harm, school shootings, and other serious topics; as well as racial slurs, sexist or homophobic statements, and any kind of discrimination.

5.2 Don't spam within channels, this can mean:
  - Chat Flood: Typing separate lines very quickly
  - Wall Text: Typing out large blocks of text
  - Repetitive Messages: Posting the same images/emojis/message multiple times
  - Epileptic Emotes: Posting/reacting with flashy GIFs or Emotes

5.3 Stay on topic —
Read the channel topics/description to understand what you can/cannot do in the channel. Bot commands should be used in their respective channels. If commands are related to the topic they can be used as long as they aren't being spammed.
